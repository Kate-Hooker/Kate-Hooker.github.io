Margins ⭒ Borders ⭒ Padding

HTML formatting often uses boxes and grids to help elements fit together.


Margins, borders and padding are all part of the vocabulary we use to refine these objects.

A border wraps around the periphery of an object. It defines the exact parameters of the shape. HTML allows you to define how thick it is, its colour and what style of line you want. Boxes often have some content inside them- padding describes how much space is left between the content and the border.
